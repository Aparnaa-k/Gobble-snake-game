Instructions:

1) Starting Position: The game begins with the snake located in the center square of the play field, and an egg appears randomly in one of the other squares.

2) Controls: Use the arrow keys (up, down, left, and right) or on-screen touch controls to move the snake in the corresponding direction.

3) Collecting Eggs: Guide the snake to move over the eggs to collect them. Each egg consumed makes the snake grow longer. Movement: The snake can only move to adjacent squares in the grid (up, down, left, and right). Plan your moves carefully to avoid obstacles and self-collision.

4) Obstacles: Watch out for obstacles or barriers that may appear in the grid as you progress through the game. Colliding with obstacles or the boundaries of the grid ends the game.

5) Scoring: Your score increases with each egg consumed. You can also set a target score for players to reach to win the game.

6) Winning: To win the game, either reach the specified target score or guide the snake to a certain length by eating a specific number of eggs.

7) Losing: The game ends if the snake collides with an obstacle, runs into the grid's boundaries, or reaches a predetermined losing condition (e.g., hitting a specific length without reaching the target).

8)Restart/Quit: Players can restart the current level or choose to quit and return to the main menu as needed. High Score: Keep track of players' high scores so they can compete for the best score.
